Waiting for Employee Enrollment

Once you add employee information, CalSavers will notify your employees, starting a 30-day decision window. During this time, employees can adjust account defaults or opt out. Any communication to your employees keeps them informed and confident in their next steps.

The 30-day waiting period begins after submitting your employee roster

Your employees have 30 days to opt out or change their contribution rate. While you wait, tell your employees that your company is joining CalSavers and prepare to add deductions in your payroll system.

Help keep your employees informed of the program during the wait

You don't need to take any action during the 30-day decision window. To ease the transition, consider emailing or sharing the Saver Brochure with your employees. This helps inform them that CalSavers will be reaching out about the program, which is crucial since many people are wary of unsolicited emails.
