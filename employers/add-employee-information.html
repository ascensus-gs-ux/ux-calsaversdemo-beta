Employee Eligibility and Requirements for the Program

Eligible employees must be employed in the state of California, be age 18 or older, and have a valid Social Security Number or an Individual Taxpayer Identification Number.

Let your employees know about the Saver's Tax Credit

Informing your employees about the Saver's Tax Credit can be a great way to enhance their awareness of available benefits. This not only helps them but also boosts overall employee satisfaction and retention.

You will need the following information all eligible employees:

  • Social Security Number (SSN) or Individual Taxpayer Identification Number (ITIN)

  • First & last name

  • Birthdate

  • Physical address

  • Phone number and email address (if available)

    An email address is the preferred form of communication as it reduces paper expense and eliminates delays associated with ground mail.

Log In and Start Add Employee Process

Log In

2

Select a Pay Schedule

  1. Choose the pay schedule group and pay schedule from the dropdown menus.
  2. Click Next

3

Download & Prepare Template

  1. Download the template to access the Excel file
  2. Within the file, review the "Formatting Examples" tab for required field guidance. See additional Tips for using the file upload template
  3. Complete all fields marked with an asterisk (*) without modifying the original formatting of the structure.
Download Excel Template

Warning! Do not modify the Excel file's formatting (columns, widths, or structure), as changes may cause errors when uploading, and cause processing delays. Tips for using the file upload template.

4

Enter Employee Information

  1. Go to the "Payroll Census" tab in the Excel file
  2. Click Enable Editing and carefully enter employee details for this payroll group
  3. Add valid email addresses when available
  4. Save your completed file.

5

Upload File in the Portal

  1. Return to the Employer Portal
  2. Drag and drop your file or click Browse to upload
  3. Click Next

6

Review & Resolve Errors (If Any)

  1. Review your employee list under Employees for accuracy
  2. Confirm employees are on the correct pay schedule
  3. If errors occur:
    • Compare your file to the template formatting
    • Correct and re-upload as needed
    • Contact Client Services if issues persist

1

Log In and Start Add Employee Process

  1. Log in to the Employer Portal
  2. Go to Step 2 – Add employee information
  3. Click Add employees
  4. Select Add manually
  5. Click Next
Log In

2

Select a Pay Schedule

  1. A new window will open to send contributions
  2. If you have multiple pay schedules, select the correct one
  3. Enter the pay date for the payroll you are reporting
  4. Choose Add Manually as your entry method
  5. Click Next

3

Enter, Review & Submit Employees

  1. Enter the amount deducted from each employee's paycheck for the pay cycle
  2. Repeat for all participating employees under the selected pay schedule
  3. Click Next once all amounts are entered

What Happens Next:

After you have added your employee information, CalSavers will then notify your employees about the program by sending a welcome packet (either by email or mail), initiating the 30-day decision window. Your employees may make changes to the account defaults or opt out of the program during this time.

During this period while you wait on employees to enroll, there are a few tasks you can complete.

Enter, Review & Submit Employees

  1. Enter details for each eligible employee in this payroll group
  2. Include an email address when available to reduce paper notices
  3. Repeat until all employees are added
  4. Carefully review all information for accuracy
  5. Click Finish and submit
  6. Do not navigate away before submitting to avoid losing your work
  7. (Optional) Download the list for your records and confirm employees are on the correct pay schedule
