Submitting Contributions

To complete the CalSavers onboarding process, you will need to send your first employee contributions to CalSavers so that the amount can be invested in their CalSavers Roth IRAs. Information can be entered either manually by updating on the portal or with file upload.

File Upload

Securely upload a standardized payroll file containing employee contribution details for each pay period.

  • Saves time with bulk uploads for each pay period

  • More consistent and accurate with standardized files

  • Scales easily for larger teams and regular submissions

Manual Upload

Log into the CalSavers portal each pay period and enter employee contributions by the scheduled due date.

  • Simple to use with no setup required

  • Full control to review and adjust each submission

  • Flexible for small teams or irregular payrolls

Looking for a more hands-off approach?

Payroll integration options can help automate the ongoing tasks of sending contributions and making updates when employees update their contribution rates.

How to submit employee contributions

File Upload

Need a more detailed walk-through?

Toggle Detailed Instructions

Watch a video on how to upload a file to submit employee contributions

1

Access the Contributions Page

  1. Log in to the Employer Portal and navigate to your Dashboard
  2. Under "Take the Next Step", locate Step 4 – Send employee contributions
  3. Click Send contributions to open the Contributions page
  4. In the Upcoming Contributions section, select Send Contributions to begin
  5. A new window will open for submitting contributions
Log In

2

Select Pay Schedule & Upload Method

  1. If you have multiple pay schedules, choose the correct one for this submission
  2. Enter the pay date that corresponds to the payroll you are reporting
  3. Under contribution entry options, select Upload File
  4. Click Next to proceed

3

Download, Complete, and Upload Template

  1. Click Download Template to access the Excel file (pre-filled with your employee list)
  2. Enter contribution amounts only for employees who are actively participating
  3. Input the amount deducted from each employee's paycheck for that pay cycle
  4. Complete all required fields (marked with an asterisk *)
  5. Save your updated file, then upload it to the Employer Portal
  6. Enter the total contribution amount you are submitting in the provided field
  7. Click Next to continue

Tip: Save a copy of your completed template to reuse each pay period—just update amounts or employee participation as needed.

4

Review, Confirm, and Submit Payment

  1. Review the contribution summary screen and confirm the total amount is correct
  2. Add or update your banking information for sending the contributions. Click "Change" if you need to update your payment method.
    • If you are paying with your business bank account include routing number, name, account number and account type.
    • Or select an alternative payment method from either "Send money via ACH", "wire funds" or "check." For check, add your check's routing number, bank name and account number. Click "Mail a paper check" if you are sending a physical check to CalSavers.
  3. Click Save after you have entered all your payment information. Then click to check the box next to "I agree to send this amount to CalSavers."
  4. At the bottom of the screen you will see in green "Show all employee contribution amounts if you want to confirm the details."
  5. Select "Send payment" to complete the task.

Additional Information

You will continue to send employee contributions each pay period. Contributions must be sent within 7 days of taking the money out of your employees' paychecks. If employees make a change to their contribution rate or choose to opt out, you will receive an e-mail or notification in the Employer Portal (you will be prompted to select your notification preference) prior to each pay period so you can make the appropriate change prior to submitting that payroll's contributions. Also, you can always access the most up-to-date information on the employer portal.

For more information on how to identify eligible employees for sending contributions, watch the video Identifying Eligible Employees.

If you need help, contact your field representative at fieldsupport@calsavers.com.
