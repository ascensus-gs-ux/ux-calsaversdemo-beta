Eligibility and Registration

Who Must Register:

Eligible employers with at least one employee in the current year are required to register or request an exemption if they meet one of the conditions for exemption.

Program Exemptions:

  • Company already sponsors a qualified retirement plan;
  • Business is closed or was sold;
  • Company does not have any employees other than the owner(s);
  • Company's classification is either a government entity, religious organization, or tribal organization

How to Register for CalSavers

Need a more detailed walk-through?

Toggle Detailed Instructions

Go to the registration or request exemption page where you will enter your Federal Employer Identification Number or Tax Identification Number (EIN/TIN) and your CalSavers Access Code. Request an Access Code if you do not have one.

Get Started

A new page will open where you will enter your company's name and address, then click Next to determine whether your business is exempt from the mandate.

Determine whether your business is exempt from the mandate by choosing your business type. If you don't see your exemption reason listed, you can request an exemption by emailing calsavers@treasurer.ca.gov.

If your company is not exempt, you'll continue through steps 4 and 5 of the registration process.

Provide contact information for the person who will run the CalSavers program for your company (Account Manager) and anyone else who may be assisting (Administrator).

Then choose a username, password, and security questions for future logins.

Review your information to make sure everything is correct, then click Submit to complete your registration. You'll receive a confirmation email with the next steps.

What to do after registration:

After registering, you will be required to complete your account setup and upload your employee roster within 30 days. CalSavers will then notify your employees about the program, initiating a 30-day opt-out window.

How to Request an Exemption for CalSavers

Need a more detailed walk-through?

Toggle Detailed Instructions

Go to the registration or request exemption page where you will enter your Federal Employer Identification Number or Tax Identification Number (EIN/TIN) and your CalSavers Access Code. Request an Access Code if you do not have one.

Get Started

A new page will open where you will enter your company's name and address, then click Next to determine whether your business is exempt from the mandate.

Choose the reason that applies to your business from the list of qualifying exemptions. If you don't see your exemption reason listed, you can request an exemption by emailing calsavers@treasurer.ca.gov.

Review your information to make sure everything is correct, then click Submit to continue to instructions to submit any supporting documentation based on your exemption selection.

If required, follow the online instructions to provide proof of existing retirement plan or other supporting documents. You'll receive a confirmation email with your exemption status.
